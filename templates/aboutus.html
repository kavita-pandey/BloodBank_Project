ABOUT US

The initiative,"Donate Blood-Save Life" led by our team is mainly aimed at facilitating the availability of blood, whether urgent or not. Blood is the basic requirement that can only be fulfilled by donation and hence, we are providing a platform that allows users to act as donors as well as receivers depending on the need.

Donors can directly be contacted by the patients in need of urgent blood and list of blood banks is also attached for reference. If you are a healthy individual and physically capable of donating blood, then why not do something good for someone? After all it feels good to save someone's life, isn't it? If you have an ill family member in an emergency, don't hesitate to call a donor or make a request for it.

We guarantee full support and one of the quickest services you may get. We are driven by our passion to serve for the greater good.

For any query regarding any of our services, reach out to us at blood.bank@gmail.com.

Never hesitate to ask for help, because for you there is always someone out there.
